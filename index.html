What I do

Front-End

I design and build eye-catching, intuitive, and responsive web applications with the use of both React.js and vanilla JS. Lately, I have been working with Typescript on a few projects, and I see why it is in high demand on large commercial projects. I have used tools such as Bootstrap 5 and Material.ui to build MVPs, but my best work is when I flesh out the visuals with my style using CSS or SCSS.

Back-End

All my main projects have a backend to make them come alive in the hands of users. I primarily use Node.js and Express, and I also have experience with Ruby on Rails. PostgreSQL (SQL) is a great database technology and it is why it is often my number one choice, but I won't shy away from working with MongoDB (NoSQL) if it makes more sense for the project.

Testing

Every good project needs robust testing to improve the quality of the development process, to eliminate as many bugs and other mistakes could otherwise be very costly if an end-user were to stumble on it. I develop my projects with testing tools such as Mocha, Chai, Jasmine, Jest, and last but not least Cypress.
